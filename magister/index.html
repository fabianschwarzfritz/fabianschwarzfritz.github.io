About me

Well, here we go: I always loved my computer and started hacking software with the age of 15. Then I got into Computer Science and finished my bachelors in a special program with several internships at SAP. After my bachelor I went to the silicon valley for 6 months to learn from the bests and embraced the silicon valley startup culture. Lost my heart in San Francisco. But now I am in Heidelberg and I am joining the next adventure: The startup getsafe.de.
